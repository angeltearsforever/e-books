ko.yaa.nis.qatsi (from the Hopi language), n. 1. crazy life 2. life in turmoil 3. life disintegrating 4. life out of balance 5. a state of life that calls for another way of living

We made this eZine at the start of Gemini Season 2020

Caroline Belle Stewart’s stories can or soon will be found in Fairy Tale Review, Black Warrior Review, Quarterly West, Hobart, No Tokens, and elsewhere. She is the co-author of a deck of narrative birding flashcards called "Mast Year: A Mystical Field Guide" (Mount Analogue). A recipient of a fellowship from the MacDowell Colony, she lives in Northampton, MA.

Caroline Rayner is a poet from Virginia who currently lives in Western Massachusetts. She is an MFA candidate at UMass-Amherst as well as assistant poetry editor for Cosmonauts Avenue. Her work has appeared or is forthcoming in Alien She, H_NGM_N, Witch Craft, Shabby Doll House, and elsewhere. Follow her on Twitter & Tumblr.
