Dedicated to Nicolas Cage

Poem by Patty Gone

April-May 2020

A man is attractive

at only two moments

In his entire life

One

When he smiles nice

As a boy

Asking ‘Is this record any good?’

A white T-shirt

Under his beige Henley

He doesn’t know

How to curl this dimples

As a weapon

Teen girls know this

Better than anyone

A boy is better

When he’s romantic enough

To play dress-up

In a four minute dance montage

With a mannequin

When he’s a virgin

Because he’s waiting

For you

He will never be more hot

Than the moment before

He learns to hide

His playfulness

Or two

He takes a bullet

For a movie star

A grizzled sheepdog

With a bad haircut

Who springs from backstage

To bleed for you

He will never be more hot

A cowboy is strong

For his own sake

But the weak man

Knows the trade

Is bullshit

Aiming an arrow

On fire

At the camera

Is bullshit

The only way

For a man to be hot

Is to love so hard

And lose it

That his gender cracks

He lets go of trying

To live life

As a sports metaphor

Soccer moms know this

Better than anyone

A man peaks

The moment he forgets

To be embarrassed

For losing
